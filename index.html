FAQ's

You've got questions, and we have the answers.

How long does a Salesforce implementation take?

A Salesforce implementation can take a couple of weeks or several months depending on the size of your business and the scope of the project. We take our time performing an expert implementation while also remaining efficient in order to deliver high quality work

What’s the difference between Salesforce and other CRM softwares?

Salesforce is an established full feature CRM that offers a wide variety of solutions for sales, service, marketing, and beyond that can be customized and scaled to meet the needs of both small and large businesses. With over 20 years in business, Salesforce continues to make breakthrough technology, such as blockchain and artificial intelligence, easy and accessible for everyone. It also offers clients the access to its immense App marketplace, which makes installing third-party integrations relatively painless. Salesforce also provides a free online website called Trailhead that includes modules and videos to help you learn how to navigate the platform.

Will my data be secure in Salesforce?

Yes, Salesforce uses a variety of methods including server authentication, data encryption, and some of the most advanced technology for Internet security available today, to ensure that your data is always safe, secure, and available only to registered users within your organization. Plus, keep your data forever with auto back-ups.

Does Salesforce work for small businesses?

Yes, Salesforce’s products scale easily to meet the needs of any size of business from sole proprietorships to global enterprises. Enjoy the benefits and advantages of every Salesforce product regardless of your size.
